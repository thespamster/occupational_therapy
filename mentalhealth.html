What Do I do?

I support individuals with mental health conditions by providing practical strategies to manage symptoms and improve daily functioning. This may be in conjunction with your treating doctor and/or psychologist depending on your individual circumstances. This support could be through helping you to establish structured routines, promoting healthy lifestyle choices, engaging in meaningful activities, enhancing your sense of purpose and achievement

Supporting Adolescents

Occupational therapists play a vital role in supporting adolescents facing mental health challenges, offering tailored interventions to help them navigate the complexities of growing up. During the teenage years, individuals often experience heightened stress due to academic pressures, social dynamics, and the transition towards adulthood. Mental health issues such as anxiety, depression, and behavioral disorders can significantly disrupt their daily lives and overall well-being. Occupational therapists work with adolescents to develop coping strategies, enhance their self-esteem, and build essential life skills.

Empowering Adults

For adults, mental health challenges such as anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia can interfere with daily functioning, relationships, and career performance. Occupational therapists support adults by helping them develop practical skills to manage their symptoms and enhance their quality of life.

How Does It Work?

Please feel free to email me, call me or WhatsApp me if you or your teenage child is experiencing mental health challenges that are disrupting the practical elements of daily life. Whether you or your teenager is dealing with anxiety, depression, work/school stress, or other mental health concerns, please don't hesitate to contact me to discuss how I can help you navigate these challenges and enhance your quality of life. Taking that first step can make a significant difference towards better mental health. I will be in touch with you within 24 hours to have a brief discussion and gather some preliminary information and set up an appointment.
