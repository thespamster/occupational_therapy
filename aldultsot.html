Vocational Services

Unique OT focuses on providing services to individuals with mental health conditions and support for employers managing employees with mental health conditions in the workplace. These services include return to work programmes, incapacity and absenteeism assessments, functional capacity evaluations and mediation services.

Return to work/ Case Management Services

Return to work and case management services are designed to support individuals transitioning back into the workplace after a period of absence due to mental health conditions. These programmes are tailored, taking into account the unique needs and circumstances of the individual, such as flexible work hours, gradual re-entry, and access to mental health resources. Collaboration between Unique OT, the employer and employee is crucial to ensure a supportive environment that promotes recovery and well-being. These programmes aim to reduce stigma, prevent relapse, and help employees regain confidence and productivity while balancing their mental health needs.
